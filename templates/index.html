The Great Migration Guide
Your playlist's journey from Spotify to YouTube Music (no passport required)
  1. Get YouTube Music Headers: Use the ytmusicapi setup tool to get your headers file (think of it as your music's visa)
  2. Upload Headers: Upload your YouTube Music headers JSON file below (the digital equivalent of showing your ID)
  3. Enter Spotify URL: Paste your Spotify playlist URL (the address of your musical homeland)
  4. Optional: Add Spotify API credentials for better performance (like having VIP access)
  5. Transfer: Click the transfer button and wait for completion (your music's flight to freedom)
